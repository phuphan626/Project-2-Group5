Pitching Visualizations

A story written in data

These interactive charts show the relationships and changes over time of a few popular pitching metrics.
Click on the colored bars to toggle the graph to reflect the metric you wish to see.
Refer to the legend for in-depth information about how these metrics are calculated.


Legend

  • Team = The Number of Teams in the League
  • R/G = Runs per Game (Total Runs/Total Games)
  • ERA = Earned Run Average (9 * ER/IP)
  • HR = Total Homeruns/Total Games
  • SO/9 = Strikeouts per 9 Innings

Games Finished

  • This graph shows the percentage of games finished by a relief pitcher or closer.
  • Hover over the individual bars to see the year and the percentage of games finished by a reliever/closer
  • The labeled boxes can be clicked to remove years as you please for comparing and contrasting different time periods
  • In 2019, 99% of games were finished by someone other than the starting pitcher. This means that only 1% of games were complete games thrown only by the starter.
